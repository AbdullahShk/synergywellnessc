About Us

South Florida's Integrative Mental Health Center

Getting help shouldn't be complicated. At Synergy Wellness Centers, we believe the first step toward healing should feel supported, not overwhelming. Our team makes it easy to find the right provider, verify your insurance, and start care — often within the same week.

We practice whole-person care — addressing all seven dimensions of well-being: emotional, environmental, physical, intellectual, financial, social, and spiritual. Our integrative approach is complemented by advanced tools like Virtual Reality therapy and biofeedback, and every care plan is available fully in-person or via telehealth.
